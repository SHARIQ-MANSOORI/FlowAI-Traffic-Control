# 🚦 FlowAI – Smart Traffic Control & Emergency Response System

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://flow-ai-traffic-control--dinoj9191.replit.app/

---

## 📌 Overview

**FlowAI** is an AI-powered traffic management system designed to optimize urban traffic flow and prioritize emergency vehicles using intelligent signal control.

It dynamically adjusts traffic signals based on real-time traffic density and creates **AI-driven green corridors** for ambulances and emergency services.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Traditional traffic systems:

* Use fixed timers ❌
* Do not adapt to real-time congestion ❌
* Delay emergency vehicles ❌

👉 Result:

* Increased traffic congestion
* Delayed emergency response
* Higher risk to human lives

---

## 💡 Solution

FlowAI solves this by:

* Dynamically adjusting signal timings
* Detecting traffic density in real-time
* Creating **automated green corridors** for emergency vehicles

---

### 🚦 AI Traffic Optimization

3. Adjusts signal timing dynamically

5. Activates green corridor across route
